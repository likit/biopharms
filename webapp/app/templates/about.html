Introduction

Biopharmaceuticals represent an interesting class of therapeutic medicinal agent produced from biological sources. Thusfar, very little information on the current status and state-of-the-art of the biopharmaceutical industry and research in Thailand is known. The value chain of the biopharmaceutical industry in Thailand is comprised of the following: research and development, pre-clinical studies, clinical trials and approval, manufacturing as well as marketing and sales.

In order for the government to boost biopharmaceutical industry in Thailand, it is critical to have a good understanding of the current state of each value chain in terms of human capital and resources to support the decision making of the policy makers.

Objective

This project aims to make sense of the compiled data of researchers working in the field of biopharmaceutical by analyzing and identifying the key players in the field, analyzing their co-authorship collaboration network, identifying potential collaborators as well as reporting the key figures and numbers of the biopharmaceutical industry in Thailand.
